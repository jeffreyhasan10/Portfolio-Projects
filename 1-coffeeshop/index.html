Our Story

In the heart of Kanyakumari, Lerey's Cafe was born from a dream to create a warm and inviting space where friends could gather over exceptional coffee and delectable treats. Founded by the Lerey family in 2024, our cafe has become a beloved local gem, known for its carefully crafted beverages and commitment to using only the finest, ethically-sourced ingredients.

Perfect Place

Coffee

At Lerey's Cafe, we believe that every cup of coffee should be a delightful experience. That's why we carefully source our beans from the finest growers around the world and meticulously roast them to perfection, ensuring a rich, full-bodied flavor in every sip. Whether you prefer a bold, robust blend or a smooth, light roast, our skilled baristas will craft the perfect beverage just for you.
